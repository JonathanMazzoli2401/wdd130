History

Our journey began amidst the roar of the river and a passion for adventure. From our first paddles to the boldest descents, Charrúa Adventure has guided explorers of all ages through the country’s most iconic rapids. Our mission is simple: to provide authentic experiences, top-notch safety, and memories as powerful as the river itself,ensuring that every journey is as unforgettable as the landscapes we traverse.

Over the years, we have built a strong community of adventurers united by their love for nature and teamwork. Each expedition is an opportunity to discover breathtaking scenery, overcome unique challenges, and create bonds that last a lifetime. At Charrúa Adventure, we believe every trip down the river is more than just an activity,it is an experience that leaves a lasting impression and inspires us to seek new adventures together.
