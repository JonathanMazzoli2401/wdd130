Las Piedras

Las Piedras, located in Canelones near Montevideo, is a historic city known for the 1811 Battle of Las Piedras and its iconic commemorative obelisk. It is recognized as Uruguay’s Capital of Grapes and Wine, standing out for its wine production and traditional annual festival. The city has important health services, educational institutions, and a train station connecting it to the capital, blending heritage, culture, and development.
